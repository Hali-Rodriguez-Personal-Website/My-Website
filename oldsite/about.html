About me

Creative hobbies outside of work I enjoy


ART

I've always drawn art since I was a young kid. I would sketch on homework and notes, and occasionally paint. Once I reached Junior year of high school I had the opportunity to take a 3D animation class through a regional occupational center. There I learned how to animate using Autodesk Maya as well as other skills through photoshop! I took the class again for my senior year but unfortunately lost many opportunities due to covid. To turn my outlook around I took individual classes during quarantine for Zbrush, Photoshop and Maya to get a better grip on the softwares. Zbrush, a 3D sculpting software, turned out to be a software I’ve grown quite fond of and the one I have been working with the most these past few years. Today it is a hobby and skill that I hope I can utilize in a professional setting.


Music

I play 4 instruments! The bass, guitar, ukulele and piano. All are self taught with the exception of a few piano lessons. I first started with a ukulele I had been gifted on christmas. My dad said, “you're never gonna play, it’s just going to collect dust”. So out of spite I taught myself ukulele! From there I picked up the guitar, then piano and most recently the bass which is one of the instruments I wanted to learn the most!


Video Games

Growing up I was always surrounded by video games on consoles like the xbox where I played the popular game series 'Halo' with my cousins. As I grew older I got into more Rpg maker and Open world games. My interest in games and art let me into wanting to work in game development which is one of my greatest aspirations today!
